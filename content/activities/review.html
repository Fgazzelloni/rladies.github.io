R-Ladies Global has a system for reviewing applications for scholarships and other types of proposals related to R (e.g., for talks or presentations at conferences) before they are submitted for competitive selections (e.g., conferences, funding, etc). The system has 80 international reviewers that provide constructive feedback, encouraging and helping submitters to improve their chances of having their work selected. Thus far, there were 27 submissions reviewed by this system.
